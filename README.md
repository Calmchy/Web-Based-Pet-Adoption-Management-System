# 🐾 AdoptMe: Web-Based Pet Adoption Management System

> A centralized platform for managing pet adoption processes efficiently and securely.

---

## 📌 Background

Pet adoption centers often rely on manual or semi-digital systems to manage pet records and adoption processes. These methods can lead to inefficiencies such as data redundancy, slow processing of applications, and difficulty tracking applicant status.

**AdoptMe** addresses this by providing a centralized web-based platform where administrators can manage pet listings and adoption applications, while adopters can easily browse pets and apply online — improving the overall adoption workflow and enhancing user experience.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

The current pet adoption process in many shelters lacks an efficient and centralized system for managing pet records and adoption applications, resulting in delays, data inconsistency, and difficulty tracking applicant status.

Specifically, this system aims to address:

- Manual handling of pet and applicant records
- Lack of a centralized database system
- Difficulty in tracking application status
- Inefficient communication between adopters and administrators
- Time-consuming adoption process

---

## 🎯 Objectives

**General Objective**

To develop a web-based pet adoption management system that improves the efficiency of managing pet records and adoption applications.

**Specific Objectives**

- Design a user-friendly registration and login system
- Allow administrators to manage pet listings
- Enable adopters to submit adoption applications online
- Provide a system for reviewing and approving applications
- Track the status of adoption applications
- Maintain a structured and secure database

---

## 🌐 Scope & Limitations

### ✅ In Scope
- Designed for a specific pet adoption center
- Supports administrator and adopter roles
- Online pet browsing and application submission
- Pet records and applicant data management
- Application status tracking
- Centralized database system

### ❌ Out of Scope
- Final onsite adoption process
- User-to-user pet selling or marketplace
- Payment processing
- Access without internet connection or registration

---

## 👥 Target Users

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|-----------------|
| **Administrator** | Manage pet listings and review/approve applications |
| **Registered Adopter** | Browse pets, submit and track adoption applications |
| **Adoption Center Staff** | Assist in day-to-day operations |
| **System Administrator** | Maintain system and database integrity |

---

## 📍 Adoption Process Note

> ⚠️ This system supports the **online pre-screening process only.**

The following steps are still completed **onsite at the adoption center:**

- Identity verification
- Document signing
- Final approval
- Pet release
